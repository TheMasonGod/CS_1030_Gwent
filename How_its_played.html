The goal of gwent is win through making the other forfit or best two out of three rounds. Gwent is a card game designed for two players. The aim of the game is to beat your opponent by ensuring that the total strength of your cards on the board exceeds that of your opponent. Rounds only end when someone passes and they stop playing more cards for the rest of that current round.

Beginning Hand

Both players draw ten cards from their deck after its been shuffled and then has the ability to put two of those cards back in the deck and draw two different cards

Combat Rows

Unit cards can be placed in one of three corresponding rows, close combat, ranged comabat, or siege

Special Cards

Special cards include faction leaders, unit abilities, weathers effects and more

Life

Each player has two lifes points at the beginning of the game. For each round lost that play loses a life, the game ends when a player loses both of their lifes causing the other play to win the game

Drawing Cards

Cards can be drawn by playing a spy card which will give the other player points and will allow the person who played the card to draw two cards. An extra card can also be drawn by at the end of won round the Norhtern Realms faction.
