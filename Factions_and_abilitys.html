There are 5 factions to choose from and every faction has the same base set of cards and some unique cards special to each faction. There are overEach faction has between 4 leader cards to choose from but only one leader can used per player in a game. Every deck/faction has a passive ability that will be used throughout the game. The list of the factions and their abilities are listed below.

  • Norhtern Realms - Grants an extra cards upon winning a round

  • Nilfgaardian Empire - Wins any round that ends in a draw

  • Scoia'tael - Decides who takes first turn

  • Monsters - Keeps a random Unit Card out after eacher round

  • Skellige - 2 random cards from the graveyard are places on the battlefield at the start of the third round
