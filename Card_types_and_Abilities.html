There are 4 main card types in Gwent; There is close combat, ranged combat, seige, and then special cards. The special cards have the ability to change the tide of battle from causing poor weather that debuffs all of the units to destroying sellect targets.

Special Cards

  • Decoy: The decoy has the ability to pick up any card on your side of the battlefield that isn't a hero cards.

  • Commander's horn: The horn can be placed the left side of any combat row and will double the points of everything on the row.

  • Scorch: This card will destory the strongest card or cards on the battle field from either side, depending on who has the strongest card on the field.

  • Biting Frost: Is a weather effect that turns all of the close combats units power to 1 on both sides of the board with the exception of hero's. The horn buff will still double the units score when debuffed but only to the next highest number.

  • Impenetrable Fog: Is a weather effect that turns all of the range combats units power to 1 on both sides of the board with the exception of hero's. The horn buff will still double the units score when debuffed but only to the next highest number.

  • torrential Rain: Is a weather effect that turns all of the seige units power to 1 on both sides of the board with the exception of hero's. The horn buff will still double the units score when debuffed but only to the next highest number.

  • Clear Weather: This will clear any and all weather effects that are currently active on the battle field.

Unit abilities

  • Medic: This ability allows to revive other unit cards that have been placed in the graveyard except hero's and special cards

  • Morale Boost: This card gives a plus 1 to every unit in the row with execpt itself and heros

  • Muster: Find any cards with the same name in your deck and play them instantly.

  • Spy: this card is placed on your opponents battlefield givning them power and the person that played it draw 2 cards from your deck.

  • Tight Bond: This unit next to a card with the same name will double the strength of both cards.

  • Agile: This unit has the flexibility to be placed on either close combat or ranged rows
